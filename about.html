About the Project

Citizen HelpDesk is a centralized platform designed to empower citizens to raise and track grievances related to public services, ensuring their voices are heard with transparency and accountability.

Our Mission

To bridge the communication gap between the public and governing authorities by providing a user-friendly complaint resolution system.

Vision

We envision a responsive, transparent, and efficient grievance redressal mechanism that helps build trust between citizens and the administration.

Key Features

  • Easy and secure complaint registration process
  • Real-time complaint tracking
  • Organized complaint categories for clarity
  • Feedback collection to improve service quality
  • Email/SMS notifications for status updates

Our Impact

By offering a single-window platform, we aim to reduce bureaucracy, ensure quicker resolutions, and foster responsible governance. Our goal is to make every citizen feel heard and respected.

Who Can Use This?

  • Residents reporting municipal or civic issues
  • Students addressing college/university concerns
  • Anyone with a grievance related to public services

Together, let’s build a more transparent and responsive system — one complaint at a time.
