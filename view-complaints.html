Understanding Complaint Statuses:

  • Pending: Your complaint has been received and is awaiting review by the relevant authorities.
  • In Progress: Action is being taken to address the issue reported in your complaint.
  • Resolved: The reported issue has been addressed and the complaint is considered closed.
  • Rejected: The complaint has been reviewed and deemed invalid or outside the scope of our services.
